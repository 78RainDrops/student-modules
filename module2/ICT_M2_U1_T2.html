Assembling a Computer

INTRODUCTION

Now that you have learned how to disassemble a PC, we are now going to proceed to assembling what we just disassembled. Ideally, as seen in the last part of the disassembly video, you need to document the specifications/status of the components before putting back the system unit together again.


A helpful tip is since you worked your way part by part in disassembly, now you are just going to retrace your steps in assembling it.
